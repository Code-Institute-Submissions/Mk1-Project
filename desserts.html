Rhubarb Mille-feuille with rice pudding creme patissiere

Elegant and classic with a twist? Look no further this Mille-feuille is perfect for you. We have taken a household favourite of rhubarb and custard and made it something to wow!

30m
Prep
35m
Cook
1h5m
Total
Ingredients Method
  • Rice pudding Creme patissiere
    • 250ml whole milk
    • 1 vanilla pod
    • 100ml double cream
    • 100g caster sugar
    • 2 egg yolk
    • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
    • 50g pudding rice
  • Rhubarb
    • 1kg rhubarb
    • 500g caster sugar
  • Puffed wild rice
  • Puff pastry sheets
  • Micro lemonbalm (for garnish)
  • Rice pudding creme patissiere
    • Bake pudding rice with milk, cream and sugar.
    • Once rice is cooked ad to blender until smooth.
    • In a metal bowl whisk remaining sugar and egg yolks till smooth.
    • Place metal bowl over a pan with water boilng and whisk in the rice mix.
    • Decant mixture in to a piping bag and chill.
  • Rhubarb
    • Peel rhubarb (keeping aside) and dice to 1inch peices.
    • mix sugar and rhubarb and place in a cling filmed metal bowl over boiling.
    • Once tender strain liquid (keeping aside) chill the diced rhubarb.
    • Add peelings from the rhubarb to strained liquid and cook for 3mins then chill.
  • Puff pastry
    • Score puff pastry into the size rectangles you want.
    • sprinkle with sugar both sides and place on lined baking sheet with lined baking sheet on top.
    • Bake at 200c for 12 mins and cool.
  • Putting it together
    • In layers add your rhubarb and creme patissiere then puff pastry
    • Garnish with puffed rice and the rhubarb syrup saved from cooking the rhubarb
